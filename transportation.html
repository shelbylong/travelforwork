Traveling for Work

Transportation

If parking in a large parking structure or parking lot, make sure to take a picture of the location. It sounds silly, but once you have been lost in a parking labyrinth in desperate search of a rental you learn to document your location!

When driving in new cities, states, or countries try not to let your gas fall below a quarter of a tank. As the territory may be new to you, you never know when the next gas station will come along (this is especially true in Ohio).

When planning and scheduling transportation make sure you are padding the time. There were plenty of times when I did not accurately account for how long traveling would take. One of those times is depicted here inside an empty ferry (which took a half hour longer than I thought it would!). Do your due diligence beforehand so you are not scrambling later!
