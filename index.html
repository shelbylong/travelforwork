Traveling for Work

Learn helpful tips and tricks on how to travel for work efficiently and effectively, while also making time for yourself.

About

This site is dedicated to helping young professionals navigate traveling for work, most likely for the first time. As a former admission counselor I was constantly on the road. I remember thinking how helpful it would have been to have some tips and tricks on hand before setting out alone. Before my work as a counselor I had no experience with traveling and found the prospect quite daunting.

Traveling is a privilege! I want to acknowledge this up front. Employers that require travel of their employees must understand that travel experience as a prerequisite can be exclusionary. These tips are not comprehensive, but they are what I learned on the road and thought might be useful to others.
